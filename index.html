Bell Curve Poetry Generator

This app generates 7 lines of poetry. Each line will have a random number of words (between 1 and 9). Words are selected from a default list, with a preference for earlier words based on a fixed "Skew Factor" of 9. It attempts to use quantum random numbers from ANU QRNG for generation.
